# Capability Contagion Experiment

## Goals

The Capability Contagion Experiment aims to explore the phenomena where the benefits and capabilities of certain techniques or technologies can spread between individuals and groups. This experiment will investigate:

1. **Understanding Capability Spread**: Analyzing how abilities, skills, or knowledge are transmitted from person to person.
2. **Impact on Team Dynamics**: Evaluating how contagion impacts collaboration and productivity in teams.
3. **Longitudinal Studies**: Observing the long-term changes in capability levels within participant groups.
4. **Methodological Approaches**: Developing new methods and practices to effectively measure and enhance capability transfer.
5. **Innovation and Knowledge Sharing**: Encouraging innovation through shared capabilities and learning experiences across different disciplines.

By achieving these goals, the experiment endeavors to contribute valuable insights into personal and organizational development frameworks.
